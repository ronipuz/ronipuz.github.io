Roni Puž is a Croatian software developer, who uses his creativity and skills for solving world problems.

He had the privilege of working with companies such as Ericsson, ReversingLabs, Binaria and people like Ivan Krešić and Randy Čupić.

Recent Work

Osjetila Zagreba

A project whose aim is to help people of Zagreb understand the city they live in by providing them infrastructure for monitoring air quality and noise. The idea is that data is collected by people for people. A concept called crowdsensing.

Brave new world

On hackathon in HUB 385, the team presented a solution for cyclists of Zagreb. It offers routing around city according to parameters like air or track quality, route difficulty... and routing across different city events and landmarks.

GPS tracking system

A project where the main goal was to develop scalable solution for GPS tracking system, which can manage thousands of devices simultaneously. Used technology: Akka.
